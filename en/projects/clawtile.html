Featured Project / AI Hardware

ClawTile AI Hardware Project

ClawTile explores a more natural AI interaction loop: capture long-form voice input, process it with AI, and return useful results without forcing every thought through a keyboard.

Back to projects See SkillFit

What problem does it explore?

Many AI use cases begin with voice: meetings, field notes, spontaneous ideas, and long reflections. ClawTile asks what happens when AI hardware starts from that input path instead of a chat box.

My product focus

  • Define where hardware meaningfully improves the AI workflow.
  • Design the loop from input capture to AI result display.
  • Turn abstract AI capability into a physical, repeatable use case.

Short answer

ClawTile is a hardware experiment about making AI easier to invoke in real-life moments, not just on a laptop.
