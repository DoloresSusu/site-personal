Why games?

Small games are fast product tests. They reveal whether an interface has a memorable visual hook, whether feedback feels good, and whether a user wants to continue interacting.

What I am practicing

  • Shipping playful interfaces quickly with AI-assisted development.
  • Testing visual hierarchy and interaction feedback in a low-stakes format.
  • Turning abstract ideas into small artifacts that can be opened, played, and shared.

Short answer

Playful builds are my low-cost lab for product feel, creative coding, and AI-native execution speed.
